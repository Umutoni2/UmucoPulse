CulturePulse Mission

Taking action today so that Rwandan stories live, breathe, and guide the generations to come.

Mission Statement

“Should we wait for our culture to diminish and die or do we take action now?”

As a young Rwandan living outside my country, my mission is to preserve and share the beauty of my culture wherever I go. I want to make sure that our traditions, language, and values such as unity, respect, and resilience are never forgotten. I will learn more about my roots, speak Kinyarwanda proudly, and share Rwandan stories, songs, and customs with others. By doing so, I hope to keep my culture alive, not just for myself, but also for the generations that will come after me.

At CulturePulse, we choose action. Our mission is to document, celebrate, and continually evolve Rwandan cultural expressions,from oral traditions and poetry to dance, crafts, and contemporary storytelling. We work with communities, artists, and institutions to ensure that wisdom from the past is not only preserved, but actively shapes the future.

Through accessible programs, open archives, and creative collaborations, we provide platforms for emerging and established voices. We believe culture thrives when people see themselves reflected in public life, education, and the digital spaces where the next generation learns and creates.

Our Vision

We aim to preserve and amplify Rwandan cultural heritage through collaboration, creativity, and community engagement. Our vision is a world where Rwandan culture is globally recognized, continually reinterpreted by new generations, and sustainably supported by its communities.

We envision classrooms where learners study local stories alongside global literature; festivals where traditional rhythms meet modern beats; and digital archives where families can discover and contribute to their own histories. To realize this vision, we nurture talent, invest in documentation, and design programs that bridge elders and youth, local communities and diasporas, tradition and innovation.

Focus Areas

  • Launch cultural workshops in schools and youth centers
  • Create a digital archive of oral traditions, poetry, and family histories
  • Support emerging artists with micro-grants and mentorship

Every activity,whether a workshop, recording session, or festival is designed to be measurable and repeatable so successful pilots can grow into lasting impact. We partner with ministries, community leaders, and artists to make culture a living part of daily life.
