About Me

I am a Rwandan creative based in the Maurtius. My work blends performance, education, and community projects that celebrate our heritage. From hosting cultural storytelling sessions to producing short documentaries, I aim to keep our traditions vibrant and accessible.

When I’m not leading a workshop, you’ll find me recording oral histories with elders, writing poetry, or teaching young learners the steps of Intore dance. Culture is my compass,it guides the work I do and the connections I build.

My journey started by recording family histories and community songs with a simple phone. Today, I design digital archives, mentor youth teams, and coordinate cultural programs so every story has a place to live online.

  • Current base: Maurtius & Rwanda
  • Focus: Cultural education, storytelling, archive design
  • Community: Workshops, artist mentorship, youth programs
